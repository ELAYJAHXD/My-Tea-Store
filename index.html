Our Promise To You

We make sure everything we serve is fresh, tasty, and made with love. Here is what you can look forward to every time you visit:

1. Premium Loose Tea Leaves

We pick out great tea leaves from local gardens and premium estates, blending them ourselves right here in the shop every Tuesday morning.

2. Homemade Pastries

We bake our treats fresh every morning from scratch, letting the dough rest overnight to make sure your croissants are warm and flaky.

3. Cozy Seating & Wi-Fi

We have fast, free Wi-Fi and plenty of comfortable spots to get some work done, read a book, or chat with friends.

Our Drink Highlight: Classic Taro Cream Blend

We love mixing up fun and cozy flavors. Come try our creamy and distinctly nutty taro root blend, infused with premium black tea leaves and crowned with a dense layer of sweet salted cream cheese foam to brighten up your day..

We always use simple, real ingredients. Check out everything else we serve on our Menu Gallery page.

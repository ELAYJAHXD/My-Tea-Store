Our Story

How a love for simple loose leaves, tasty iced blends, and hot teapots started our little neighborhood shop.

What We Believe In

We started Kanto Tea with one easy goal: to bring great tea and a friendly vibe right to our neighborhood. We don't think a good cup of tea should be hard to find or fancy. Whether you are grabbing a quick iced milk tea on your way to work or sitting down with a warm pot of green tea, we want our shop to feel like home.

How We Make Our Drinks

We care about what goes into your cup. Here is what makes our little kitchen different:

  • No Fake Syrups: We sweeten our drinks with our own house mixers made from real brown sugar, fresh fruits, and honey.
  • Real Whole Leaves: We never use artificial flavor powders—only real tea leaves, real herbs, and real flower petals for our blends.
